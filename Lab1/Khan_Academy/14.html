Apple Cider Chicken

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
skinless, boneless chicken breasts 2
teaspoon kosher salt 1
pinch cayenne 1
sage leaves 6

Step 1: Season chicken on both sides with salt, black pepper, and cayenne and set aside..

Step 2:Melt 3 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat; add sage leaves and fry until crisp, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove and reserve.

Step 3:Add chicken breasts to the same skillet, and cook until just cooked through, about 4 minutes per side. Remove to a plate and cover loosely with foil.

Step 4:Add remaining 1 tablespoon butter to the pan, and turn off the heat. Swirl pan until butter emulsifies into the sauce. Taste for seasoning and serve on chicken, garnished with fried sage.

Carbonara Beans

  • Time:25 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
pepper bacon, chopped 4
cloves garlic, minced 2
whipping cream 1/2

Step 1:Cook bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until browned and crispy, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds more.

Step 2:Add cream and bring just to a simmer. Stir in the 1/2 cup cheese, salt, and cracked black pepper; continue cooking until lightly thickened, stirring often, about 3 minutes..

Step 3:Add beans and heat through, about 2 minutes.
